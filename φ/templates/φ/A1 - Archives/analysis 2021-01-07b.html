Internal Audit Mission
USERS
Users of the web application range from business and portfolio managers, valuators, risk managers, brokers, financial advisors and others. As the application is made available to anyone who wishes to consult the database, the users are essentially made up of the public at large.
TYPE OF AUDIT
Data Processing Internal Audit
NEEDS OF USERS
Users need Financial Statements exempt from material misstatement in order to conduct various analysis aimed at evaluating the entity's financial performance, understanding its financial and cost structure, making investment decisions and implementing appropriate strategies with full knowledge of the underlying circumstances.
SCOPE
{{ entity.column5 }} to {{ entity.column1 }}
RISK
The financial information obtained from the Security and Exchange Commission has already been duly audited. The main risk factor therefore lies in the collection and compilation of this data. Furthermore, the data subsequently used in the business valuation process is flexible and it is the responsibility of the user to ensure that it is adequate.
STRATEGY
Given the nature of the assignment, only corroboration procedures can be carried out. The main objective is to ensure the completeness and accuracy of the data.

Materiality Threshold
Target level of assurance
 95%
Calculation basis
5% of income from continuing operations
1% of revenues
1% of net asset value
Materiality Threshold
Methodology: Unless the threshold calculated on the basis of income from continuing operations is lower than one of the other methods, it is the most appropriate basis for the needs of users who essentially seek an understanding of the company's performance. Also, given that the users' focus is more specific to the most recent financial information, the values of the basis are those of the last year completed. Since the value of goodwill of listed companies is not, or only partially, taken into account in the net asset value, and this value is generally significant to them, the use of revenues as a basis for calculation is more appropriate in the second place.
