DOCUMENTATION

Search Bar

Just about 4,000 trading symbols are available in our database. With a simple request to a URL we locate the information for any specific entity. That information is most of the time partially complete as each account is given it's specific method pertaining to it's nature. Doing so requires us to reverse engineer the financial statements from the reading of the Security And Exchange Commission database, which leads to deeper understanding of the information it contains. As we go along, our AI will acquire the vocabulary necessary to read any financial statements quite fast. We expect to be able to read 80% of it all before 2024.

Sort And Filter Entities

You can access our customizable filter by pressing the φ logo from home page. Our AI will locate the entities based on the industry, region and period end date and sort them according to our valuation ratio, the Clockdb Ratio, according to its market capitalization, its intrinsic value, its industry, region, period end date or even in alphabetical order if you choose so. The information sorted will get better and better over time as our reading of the financial statements becomes more accurate. In the mean time it is still a good indicator of where to look for investment finding purposes if your willing to put in a little effort yourself.

Financial Statements

Our one-page application provides you with trial balances in a customizable grid. Everything else underlying financial statements is programmed upstream. Your financial statements won't ever break. You can access hidden rows of the General Ledger by checking Options/Settings/Unhide Empty Rows/. You can rollover the information by accessing Options/Rollover/. Our database is updated occasionally depending on available resources and the progress of research. Our focus is on providing you with a meaningful tool both in front and back end and we put in the work where we judge we add more value first.

Audit

Considering the level of precision our goal requires, we wrote Computer Assisted Audit Techniques both in Python and JavaScript. Those procedures are put in place to address the accuracy of the financial statements rebuilt in our database. To some extent, we find both true and false negative in the backend, but in doing so, we learn the vocabulary of the accounts and the totals and subtotals, which proves to accelerate the growth of our intelligence.

Business Valuation

From audited Financial Statements, we compute capitalization rates, capitalized cash flow, capitalized income, weighted intrinsic values and ratios. You can customize the information by checking Options/Settings/Open Editor/. With this information you will be able to compare the market capitalization of a trading symbol (its price) to the intrinsic value and visualize it in a simple line chart. Over time, we which to provide the general public with the generic of the fundamental valuation.

Opinion

You can dress you own opinion of the value of a stock based on both the information available in our database and any corrections or further analysis.

Files

Any worthy accounting software needs to map the files of an engagement to the Financial Statements, Business Valuation or Audit resulting from them. You can upload your files names in Options/Files/New/ and reference them to the different sections of the engagement. You can customize the Reference Map by reaching Options/Reference Map/ to make sure it matches your conventions. These files will be filtered when a reference is selected. The next step will be for us to build an executable. That way we will have local access to your desktop for better performances soon.

Save

Your work can be saved for later consultation by clicking Options/Save/. Any modification, checkmark or referencing will be stored. You can consult it again by accessing Options/Open/.

New

You can start a project from scratch by reaching Options/New/. This can be very interesting for private corporations who which to keep track of their financial position or in the context of merger, acquisition, and reorganization.

Open Source

Our code is open source. https://github.com/clockdb/clockdb.github.io.
