This element represents deferred leasing and financing costs net of accumulated amortization. Deferred leasing costs incurred by the lessor that are (a) costs to originate a lease incurred in transactions with independent third parties that (i) result directly from and are essential to acquire that lease and (ii) would not have been incurred had that leasing transaction not occurred and (b) certain costs directly related to specified activities performed by the lessor for that lease. This element is net of accumulated amortization.
