The aggregate amount of depreciation expense and amortization expense. Depreciation expense is the noncash expense recognized in the current period that reflects the allocation of the cost of tangible assets over the assets' useful lives. Amortization expense is the noncash expense charged against earnings in the current period that reflects the allocation of the cost of definite lived intangible assets and leasehold improvements over the shorter of their estimated remaining economic lives or the lease term. Excludes the amortization of deferred sales commissions.
