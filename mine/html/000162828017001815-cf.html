- Definition

Represents the amount of real estate assets that an Entity acquires in a noncash (or part noncash) acquisition. Real estate assets refer to the lands, buildings and intangible assets (including those that are part of operations and those under development) of a real estate entity, net of accumulated depreciation and amortization. Noncash is defined as information about all investing and financing activities of an enterprise during a period that affect recognized assets or liabilities but that do not result in cash receipts or cash payments in the period. "Part noncash" refers to that portion of the transaction not resulting in cash receipts or cash payments in the period.
