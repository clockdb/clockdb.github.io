Licensing revenue is consideration received from another party for the right to use, but not own, certain of the entity's intangible assets. Licensing fees are generally, but not always, fixed as to amount and not dependent upon the revenue generated by the licensing party. An entity may receive licensing fees for licenses that also generate royalty payments to the entity. Royalty revenue is derived from a percentage or stated amount of sales proceeds or revenue generated by the third party using the entity's property.
